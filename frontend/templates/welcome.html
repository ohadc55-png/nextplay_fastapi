Founder Story

Built by a Coach, for Coaches

Hi, I'm Ohad.

After 18 years as a pro player — including a journey with my hometown club Hapoel Be'er Sheva from the third division to Israel's Premier League — and 16 years coaching every level, I've learned coaching is about people, not just X's and O's. But as a Solo Coach, it's hard to stay connected. When you're managing everything alone, players inevitably slip through the cracks. It's heartbreaking to miss a moment to support a player because you were buried in technicalities.

I built NEXTPLAY to be the staff I never had — and the one you deserve. It handles the heavy lifting so you can lead with both precision and a personal touch.

Top clubs have a room full of experts. NEXTPLAY gives that same power to the rest of us, turning the 2 AM grind into a professional workflow.

Focus on your players. We'll handle the rest.
